This cross-disciplinary perspective helps me communicate effectively with designers, developers, analysts, and stakeholders alike. Whether building a system from scratch or improving an existing one, I aim to deliver solutions that perform well technically while aligning with real-world objectives and user needs.

DIGITAL STRATEGIST WITH TECH SKILLS

CREATIVE + ANALYTICAL BALANCE
